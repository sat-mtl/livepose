# Installation

## Prerequisites

- A supported platform: **macOS**, **Linux** (x86_64), or **Windows**
- A connected camera

## Download

Pre-built packages are available upon signing up to [toolbox.sat.qc.ca](https://toolbox.sat.qc.ca/) Download the artifact for your platform.
## Models

Pre-built `.onnx` models are available from the [model-storage release](https://github.com/sat-mtl/livepose/releases/tag/model-storage). Download the model(s) you want and point LivePose to them at launch.
